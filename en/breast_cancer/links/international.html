International Resources


National Comprehensive Cancer Network (NCCN®)

The NCCN is a good source for information pamphlets, guidelines and resources.

http://www.nccn.org/patients/default.aspx


Cancer Research UK
Cancer Research UK is a good source for breast cancer, as well as generalized cancer information. It also provides information for how to cope with certain side effects, including; emotional and physical coping.
http://www.cancerresearchuk.org/about-cancer/type/breast-cancer/
http://www.cancerresearchuk.org/about-cancer/coping-with-cancer/


Breast Cancer Organization
The Breast Cancer Organization provides useful general information about breast cancer.
http://www.breastcancer.org/


Young Survival Coalition
This organization is based out of the United States and targets young women affected with breast cancer. Online audio files are available from past events where speakers have spoken about topics including breast cancer types, nutrition, physical and emotional wellness and relationships.
http://www.youngsurvival.org/programs/learn/transcripts-audio-files
