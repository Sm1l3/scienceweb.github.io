Personal Care: Health, Beauty, Hair and Tips


Look Good Feel better
Look Good Feel better has a series of how-to videos and tips for personal care for women with cancer. They also provide free workshops for women and teens across Canada, where hair and cosmetic specialists help women feel more like themselves by teaching them about topics such as skin care, wigs, hair and cosmetics.
http://lgfb.ca/en/


Canadian Breast Cancer Network
The Canadian Breast Cancer Network also provides women with specific information relating to their chosen province, including the location of wig and head covering stores, mastectomy bras and prosthesis stores and support groups.
http://cbcn.ca/


Breast Cancer Organization
The Breast Cancer Organization provides women with tips on how to cope with treatment side effects, including lymphedema.
http://www.breastcancer.org/

Breast cancer care UK
Breast cancer care UK has a booklet on their website that can be ordered that is titled: breast cancer and you.
https://www.breastcancercare.org.uk/information-support/publication/breast-cancer-you-diagnosis-treatment-future-bcc44
