Topics

The themes identified by women are summarized in seven main groups. Each group has several topic pages that highlight the experiences of women such as preserving fertility or understanding the diagnosis etc. You can read more about a theme by clicking on one of the main groups and selecting the theme of your interest. This will bring you to a page with an overview followed by summaries of what women shared with relevant video, audio or textual clips from individual interviews. You can open them by clicking on the text beside the image.
