Each main group has several topic pages that each discuss a specific theme such as financial impact or providing support etc. You can read more about a theme by clicking on one of the main groups and selecting the theme of your interest. This will bring you to a page with an overview of the experiences of the caregivers relating to this theme. The page is illustrated with relevant video, audio or textual clips of the interview.
